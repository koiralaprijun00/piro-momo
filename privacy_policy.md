# Privacy Policy for Piro Momo Games

_Last updated: 2025_

Piro Momo Games (“the App”) is developed and maintained by **Prijun Koirala**. This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles user data.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App does **not** collect, store, share, or process any personal data.

We do **not** collect:

- Names  
- Email addresses  
- Phone numbers  
- Location data  
- IP addresses  
- Device identifiers  
- Gameplay analytics  

The App works fully offline and does not require login or account creation.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate or share data with any third-party services, including:

- Ad networks (e.g., AdMob)  
- Analytics tools  
- Crash-reporting services  
- External SDKs  

## 3. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected, no child-specific information is processed.

## 4. Data Security

Because the App stores no user data, no information can be lost, leaked, or misused.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

If the App begins using analytics, ads, or any third-party services in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be informed through an app update or release notes.
